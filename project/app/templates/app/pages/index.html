Let's Get Kids All In School!

{# If you support Full-Time, In-Person, K-12 Instruction you should join our movement! #}

Join Us!
Learn More

With priority-vaccination for teachers scheduled to be complete January 31, it's time to get the Kids All In school. Our objective is Full-Time, In-Person, K-12 instruction by Monday, March 1.

When you Join Us either via email, Google or Facebook, you'll become part of our community of parents, teachers, and staff who are all-in for the kids. Before every Board meeting we'll produce a report showing the updated totals of the "signatures" we've collected. (You can choose to keep your name private if you wish.)

We all want the same goal, but we've been living this "new normal" for a long time and change — even for the better — is always difficult. The Board of Trustees and District Administration need our support to accomplish this goal, and that's why we created this website.

We're not asking for donations, we won't share or sell your data, and we have no agenda beyond getting the Kids All In. You can learn more in our FAQ or ask any other questions at support@kidsallin.com. Thank you!
