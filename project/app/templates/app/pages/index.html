With priority-vaccination for teachers scheduled to be complete January 31, it's time to get the Kids All In school. Our objective is Full-Time, In-Person, K-12 instruction by Monday, March 1.

To accomplish this goal the District needs your support; we've been living this "new normal" for a long time and change — even for the better — is always difficult. That's why we created this website: to focus our voices and give the Board and Adminstration confidence towards our shared purpose.

When you Join Us via email, Google or Facebook, you'll become part of our community of parents, teachers and staff who are all-in for the kids. You can participate anonymously or publicly, and before every Board meeting we'll produce reports so Trustees can see how far our support extends.

We're not asking for donations, we won't share or sell your data, and we have no agenda beyond getting the Kids All In. You can learn more in our FAQ or ask any other questions at support@kidsallin.com.

Be All In for the Kids!
